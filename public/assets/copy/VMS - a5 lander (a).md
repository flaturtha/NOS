In an age of endless digital content, our shared literary history from the **19th and early 20th centuries** is at risk. For decades, these brilliant stories were **written off as popularist trash by the ivory tower literary elite.** Today, they are fading away—trapped in crumbling archives or existing only as **digital ghosts** in poor-quality scans.

My work begins by transforming these ghosts back into real, readable text. This is what the restoration process looks like:
